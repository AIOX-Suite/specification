## FAQ

### Q: Can I implement AIOX in my personal blog for free?
**A:** Yes! The specification is open and free to implement.

### Q: Can I build an open-source WordPress plugin that uses AIOX?
**A:** Yes! Open-source, non-commercial implementations are freely licensed.

### Q: Can I sell a commercial product that uses AIOX?
**A:** Yes, but you need a commercial license for the patented implementation methods. Contact licensing@aioxsuite.com.

### Q: Can I use AIOX in my company's internal website?
**A:** If your company is using it internally (not offering as a service), contact us to discuss licensing. Often, internal use can be accommodated affordably.

### Q: What if I just use the specification without the patented methods?
**A:** If you implement the specification using your own methods (not the patented algorithms), you only need to follow the CC BY 4.0 attribution requirements.
